About Diamond

WELCOME!
Diamond Café is a Jordanian based café, it is a warmly lit, dynamic yet relaxing coffee place whose casual vibes make it perfect for social outings, studying, business lunches, and everything in between.

The two-floor 100% smoke-free area, which is unique in the capital, is also equipped with power outlets and USB chargers, and high speed internet for guests who are studying or working.

You can find us in “ Queen Rania Street — Amman, Jordan. Opposite to Jordan University main gate “.The café' has it own car park.

Open Hours : All Days FROM ' 7:00 AM ' TO ' 12:30 AM '.
